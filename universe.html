Since the groups for this study were predetermined, it was comparable to dealing with strangers in the workplace. Creating a business concept that directly benefits the community was the project's main objective. The comfort in the physical community is decreasing due to the pandemic's aftereffects and the dominance of the internet world. When we refer to a physical community, we mean circumstances in which individuals are gathered in one location with the ability to interact and work towards a common goal. The social effects of online and distant learning have affected people's social abilities in general. Since our app is a social media platform, our approach may appear counterintuitive at first, but that's because it will be simple to draw students in the first place, and our primary goal with the app was to promote activities that would help to close the physical gap.
